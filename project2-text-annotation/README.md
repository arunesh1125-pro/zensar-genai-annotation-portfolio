# GEN AI Annotation Portfolio
### Fresher Training Project | Targeting: Zensar Technologies GEN AI Role

## About This Repository
This portfolio demonstrates hands-on data annotation skills across text annotation — core skills required for GEN AI Supporting roles in Annotation, Labeling, and AI Training.

## Project 2: GEN AI Text Annotation Suite
**Tool:** LabelStudio  
**Format:** JSON  
**Types:** NER, Sentiment, Intent, RLHF  

### Task 1 — Named Entity Recognition (NER)
- Annotated 10 sentences
- Entity types: PERSON, ORGANIZATION, LOCATION, DATE, PRODUCT
- Dataset: News and business sentences

### Task 2 — Sentiment Labeling
- Classified 10 customer reviews
- Labels: POSITIVE, NEGATIVE, NEUTRAL
- Dataset: Restaurant/product reviews

### Task 3 — Intent Classification
- Labeled 10 conversational sentences
- 9 intent categories for chatbot NLU training
- Dataset: Smart assistant commands

### Task 4 — RLHF Response Rating
- Evaluated 5 AI response pairs
- Rating criteria: Accuracy, Helpfulness, Safety, Clarity
- Directly applicable to LLM fine-tuning pipelines
